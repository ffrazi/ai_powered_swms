About Smart Waste Reporting

Smart Waste Reporting is a cutting-edge platform designed to improve waste management and environmental sustainability. Our system leverages AI technology to detect overflowing bins in real-time, alert municipal authorities, and optimize waste collection schedules.

Our goal is to create cleaner, more sustainable cities by using innovative technology to streamline waste management operations and reduce the environmental impact of overflowing bins and inefficient waste collection.
